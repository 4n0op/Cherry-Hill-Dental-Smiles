Her Story

Passionate About People, Not Just Teeth

Dr. Deepika M. Reddy brings nearly two decades of dental expertise to Cherry Hill, serving patients across Atlantic, Camden, and Ocean counties with the kind of personalized care that keeps families coming back for generations.

After earning her Bachelor of Dental Surgery from Oxford Dental College in India, she pursued her Doctor of Dental Surgery at the prestigious New York University College of Dentistry — one of the top dental schools in the world — where she developed an exceptional foundation in both clinical technique and patient communication.

Beyond her dental credentials, Dr. Reddy serves the community through the United States Army Reserve, a commitment that speaks to her discipline, integrity, and dedication to those around her. Outside the office, she channels her creative energy into travel and photography — a passion that sharpens the eye for detail she brings to every treatment.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Care Under One Roof

From your child's first cleaning to full smile makeovers, Dr. Reddy provides complete dental care for the whole family.

Family Dentistry

Preventive cleanings, exams, and education for patients of all ages. We make kids feel comfortable from day one.

Restorative Dentistry

Crowns, bridges, fillings, and full-mouth restorations to bring damaged teeth back to full health and function.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers designed to give you the confidence to show off your smile.

Emergency Dentistry

Same-day emergency slots available. Broken tooth, severe pain, or lost crown — we're here when you need us most.

Invisalign®

Clear aligner therapy for straighter teeth without metal braces. As a certified Invisalign provider, Dr. Reddy creates precise, beautiful results.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement that looks, feels, and functions like natural teeth. Dr. Reddy is a certified implant placement specialist.

FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

Can't find what you're looking for? Call us at 856-667-3737 and we'll be happy to help.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes! We welcome new patients of all ages and do our best to accommodate flexible scheduling, including same-day emergency slots for urgent needs.

Do you offer Invisalign for teens?

Absolutely. Dr. Reddy is a certified Invisalign provider and offers treatment for both teens and adults. We'll determine if you're a good candidate during a complimentary consultation.

Do you see children?

Yes — we're a kid-friendly practice with a calm, welcoming atmosphere. We enjoy helping young patients build healthy dental habits from an early age.

What are your safety and sterilization protocols?

We follow strict sterilization procedures, use hospital-grade disinfectants, and fully adhere to CDC and ADA guidelines. Your health and safety is our top priority at every visit.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major dental insurance plans and also offer flexible in-house financing options. Contact our office and we'll help verify your benefits before your appointment.

How do I schedule an emergency appointment?

Call us directly at 856-667-3737 and let us know it's an emergency. We reserve same-day slots for urgent situations and will do everything we can to see you quickly.
